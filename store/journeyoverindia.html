Product Description

Journey Over India is Paul Alexandre John's first album. The disc features Indian classical music performed by Paul, with Ray Spiegel on tabla and dhol. Two complete ragas, one for morning and one for the spring season, are showcased with over twenty minutes each of recording time, which allows for a full development of the raga. The six shorter pieces on Journey Over India present some of the many facets of the music of India -- chants, lighter pieces, and folk songs. The project took a little over one year, from initial recordings, through production and mastering to its release in 1994.
